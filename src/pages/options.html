Anki knowledge

With Anki open and AnkiConnect installed, LexiEng reads headwords and card schedules from your mining deck. It changes a schedule only when you deliberately grade a card. Add to Anki creates a new note when clicked; LexiEng never edits or deletes existing notes.

Target vocabulary

Frequency filter

Ranks at or below the known ceiling are treated as already known. Anki words are always known regardless of rank. The default excludes the first 20,000 words.

Jiten-style page workflow

Reading behavior

Parsed tokens stay in the page. Press Q over any word for the dictionary, Alt+P to parse, Alt+L to look up a selection, Alt+H for reader mode, and Alt+S for the status bar.

Anki scheduler

On-page reviews

The dictionary popup uses Anki’s own Again, Hard, Good, and Easy actions. If FSRS is enabled in Anki, these are FSRS reviews. Keys 1–4 grade the first reviewable card while the popup is open.

Focused reading

Reader mode

LexiEng extracts the largest readable article region into a clean, full-page view and immediately parses it. The configured extension theme is reused.

Quiet by design

Appearance

Flat colors, no gradients, no motion. Inter is bundled for consistent English text; Japanese definitions fall back to Noto Sans JP, Yu Gothic UI, or Meiryo.
